Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new habit?

Creating a habit in MojaNavika is simple:

  1. Open the app and tap the + button
  2. Enter a name for your habit (e.g., "Drink water", "Read 20 pages")
  3. Choose how often you want to track it (daily, specific days of the week, etc.)
  4. Optionally set a reminder notification
  5. Tap Save and you're all set

Your new habit will appear on the home screen, ready to be checked off.

How do streaks work?

A streak counts the number of consecutive days (or scheduled occurrences) you have completed a habit without missing one.

For example, if you set a daily habit and complete it every day for 10 days in a row, your streak is 10. If you miss a day, the streak resets to zero and you start building it again.

For habits scheduled on specific days (e.g., Monday, Wednesday, Friday), the streak tracks consecutive scheduled days only — non-scheduled days do not break your streak.

How do I restore my purchases?

If you have previously purchased a subscription and need to restore it (for example, after reinstalling the app or switching to a new device):

  1. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase
  2. Open MojaNavika and go to Settings
  3. Tap Restore Purchases

Your subscription status will be verified with the App Store and restored automatically. If the restore does not work, please contact us at ivan.boban@softsplit.com.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID, not inside the app. To cancel:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions
  3. Find MojaNavika in the list and tap it
  4. Tap Cancel Subscription and confirm

Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. MojaNavika is designed with a privacy-first approach:

  • All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • There are no user accounts — nothing to sign up for, nothing to leak
  • There is no server or backend — the app works 100% offline
  • We use no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs that collect data
  • We never see, access, or transmit any of your habit data
