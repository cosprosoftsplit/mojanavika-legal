Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

MojaNavika does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

MojaNavika is a privacy-first habit tracking app for iOS, developed by SoftSplit d.o.o. We believe your habits are your own business. This policy explains our approach to your data — in short, we never see it.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. MojaNavika has:

  • No user accounts or sign-in
  • No analytics or tracking tools
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • No server or backend — the app is 100% offline

Data Storage

All your habit data — including habits, completions, streaks, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

If you delete the app, all your data is permanently removed from your device.

Notifications

MojaNavika offers optional push notifications to remind you about your habits. These notifications are:

  • Entirely user-controlled — you choose if and when to receive them
  • Processed locally on your device by the operating system
  • Not routed through any external server

You can enable or disable notifications at any time through the app or your device's Settings.

In-App Purchases

MojaNavika offers optional subscriptions through in-app purchases. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. For details on how Apple handles purchase data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not have any of your data to share. There are no advertising networks, analytics services, or data brokers involved in MojaNavika.

Children's Privacy

Since MojaNavika does not collect any personal data, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
